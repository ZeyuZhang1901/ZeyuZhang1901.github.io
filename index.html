About me

I am Ph.D. student in the Department of Statistic and Data Science at Northwestern University, hosted by Dr. Ruan Feng. I received my B.Eng. in Electronic Engineering and Information Science at University of Science and Technology of China in 2023. My research interests reinforcement learning, optimization, and machine learning in general. Currently I focus on offline reinforcement learning theory and its application to recommendation and other information retrieval problems.
